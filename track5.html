SkullCandy is offering the mesh 2 headphones in a varity of colour combinations. Headphones are light weight and fit pretty snug without crushing your ears. The Hesh 2 headphones sound just as good. They are much lighter than more expensive pairs, and I still laud Skullcandy for their attention to the tensile strength of the cable coming out of the headphone.

The nice thing on these headphones is the cable is not fixed to the ear piece so any standard 3.5mm to 3.5mm jack will work.Overall, the Skullcandy Hesh 2 headphones are a great deal to buy. They retail for $60, $70 if you want a pair with a microphone.
